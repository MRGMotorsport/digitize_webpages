WHAT IS DIGITIZE?

Digitze was conceptualised as a result of wondering where has my car been and what sort of life did it have?. I found myself trawling though the service history, but found that this didnt tell the true story. I then thought, why not extend this service to other enthusiast owners? As a result, the bike and motorbike functionality was added to create the platform you see today. If you think of an item or vehicle that you think should be digitally twinned using this platform, get in touch by signing up and heading to the the contact section!

Our platform allows owners to tell the true story of their vehicle with two key aims:

  • Preserve the history of a vehicle, both service history and memories. Is your phone full of pictures of your bike in interesting places, randomly scattered across your gallery? With Digitize, all of these can be stored in one place with notes and details, as well as options for collating into a portfolio.
  • Help increase the value of the vehicle by providing a deeper understanding of the life vehicle has lived. Has it been on 17 track days and never been serviced, or has it been dry stored for a decade and only been driven for the last year. Digitize helps buyers understand how the item has been used and cared for.

The long term aim of Digitize is to have enough users to begin doing meets and events to create an enthusiast community. The founders of Digitize are not a group of investment "money-types" and the sole aim of the platform is to provide a service to people who care about their hobbies. This is why there is a free usage plan which will remain free with its capabilities never reduced, and a marginally above cost to us paid plan with increased functionality detailed below. Most importantly, we will NEVER sell your data as we beleive this to be unethical. This platform has been created by enthusiasts, for enthusiasts.

HOW CAN I GET STARTED?

We are delighted that you are interested in joining our community and we have two levels of membership to suit your needs. The first tier of membership titled "Enthusiast" is a totally free for life membership for you to store up to 4 cars, bikes, or motorbikes. You can add details of services, journeys, modifications, and many other things. This allows you to track spend and usage. The second tier titled "DIGITIZE Club Member" is a paid for option where you can store up to 10 vehicles in your digitally twinned garage. On top of the standard free features, you can add photos, access more detailed analytics, and have access to this history portfolio generator which is designed to increase the value of your garaged item upon sale. You will also have access to the vector drawing commission area should you want our lead graphic designer to create you a detailed drawing of your vehicle like you see on this homepage.
